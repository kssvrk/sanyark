The Power of Fusion

Our LEO satellites are uniquely engineered to concurrently provide resilient PNT signals and secure, mid-bandwidth data links, creating a sum far greater than its parts.

Precision PNT

Centimeter-level, high-integrity positioning and timing signals, resistant to jamming and spoofing, for critical navigation and synchronization needs.

Secure Communications

Reliable, low-latency data links optimized for M2M and IoT applications. We provide the crucial mid-bandwidth connectivity others overlook.

Unified Platform

A single, SWaP-optimized user terminal provides access to both services, reducing complexity, cost, and power consumption for any application.

The Sanyark Stack

Our innovation is driven by a deeply integrated stack of advanced technologies, designed for performance, resilience, and scalability.

Software-Defined Agility

Our satellites are built on a foundation of Software-Defined Radios, allowing us to dynamically update and reconfigure payloads in orbit. This means we can adapt to new threats, deploy new services, and optimize signal performance on the fly, ensuring a future-proof and resilient constellation.

  • In-orbit payload reconfiguration.
  • Support for multiple, custom waveforms.
  • Enhanced resilience against electronic warfare.

Multi-Beam Precision

Phased array antennas form the heart of our service delivery, enabling us to generate and steer multiple beams simultaneously from a single satellite. This technology allows for precise geographic coverage, efficient use of power, and the ability to serve many users and applications at once.

  • Serve multiple regions and users concurrently.
  • No moving parts for higher reliability.
  • Rapid, electronic beam steering.

Intelligent Signal Shaping

Advanced digital beamforming gives us unparalleled control over our PNT and Comms signals. We can shape beams to maximize signal strength for users, create nulls to mitigate interference and jamming, and ensure our services are delivered with the highest integrity and security, even in contested environments.

  • Anti-jam and anti-spoofing capabilities.
  • Power optimization for extended satellite life.
  • High-gain beams for difficult environments.

A Web in the Sky

Our constellation is interconnected with high-capacity Inter-Satellite Links, forming a resilient mesh network in space. This reduces reliance on ground stations, lowers latency for data transfer, and allows for rapid command, control, and synchronization across the entire fleet, ensuring seamless global service.

  • Ultra-low latency communication pathways.
  • Reduced dependence on ground infrastructure.
  • Self-healing, resilient network topology.

Intelligence in Orbit

By embedding AI and machine learning processors directly onto our satellites, we enable real-time, on-board data analysis and decision-making. This allows for autonomous network management, intelligent resource allocation, and processing data at the source, delivering insights faster than ever before.

  • Autonomous satellite and network operations.
  • Real-time signal quality monitoring and optimization.
  • On-board data filtering to reduce backhaul needs.
